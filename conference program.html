Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)

The conference brings together researchers, engineers, and practitioners from academia and industry to share their knowledge and expertise in areas such as robot perception, motion planning, control, and applications. ICRA is one of the premier forums for presenting and discussing the latest research results and developments in the field, as well as for networking with other professionals and stakeholders in the robotics community.
