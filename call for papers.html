Submission Process

  1. Prepare your paper according to the conference guidelines.
  2. Go to the conference submission website.
  3. Create an account if you do not have one.
  4. Submit your paper.

Submission Guidelines

  • Papers must be original and not submitted to any other conference or journal.
  • Papers must be written in English.
  • Papers must follow the IEEE conference template.
  • Papers must not exceed 6 pages in length.
  • Papers must be submitted in PDF format.

The conference is soliciting two types of contributions:

  • papers (10 pages) presenting previously unpublished research achievements or IEEE experiences and solutions
  • Posters (2 pages) showcasing early-stage results and innovations

Submitted papers should use the IEEE 8.5×11 manuscript guidelines: double-column text using single-spaced 10-point font on 8.5×11-inch pages. Note. The use of artificial intelligence (AI)–generated text in an article shall be disclosed in the acknowledgements section of any paper submitted to an IEEE Conference or Periodical. The sections of the paper that use AI-generated text shall have a citation to the AI system used to generate the text.
