Call for Papers

The IEEE Conference invites submissions of original research papers, case studies, and work-in-progress reports on the following topics:

  • IEEE Article Processing Charges
    For the majority of IEEE's fully open access journals, the article processing charge is $1,950 USD. Some exceptions apply for certain titles, see individual journal author instructions for specific details. For IEEE Access, the article processing charge is $1,950 USD.

  • Abstract and Introduction
    An IEEE conference is a gathering sponsored at least in part by some entity of the IEEE where technical information is exchanged and discussed. The gathering may be called a conference, symposium, meeting, workshop, etc. An IEEE conference proceedings is the official record of an IEEE conference meeting.
  • Guidelines for subbmission
    Manuscript should be prepared in a double column, single-spaced format using a required IEEE Access template. A Word or LaTex file and a PDF file are both required upon submission. Content on each file must match exactly.

Submissions should be in the form of extended abstracts (up to 500 words) and should clearly state the research problem, research methodology, and expected contributions. Full papers will be requested for accepted abstracts. All submissions will be subject to a double-blind review process by the Program Committee.

Important Dates:

  • Submission Deadline: May 31, 2023
  • Notification of Acceptance: July 31, 2023
  • Camera-ready Papers Due: August 31, 2023
  • Conference Dates: November 1-3, 2023
