Technical Committee

A technical team is a group of professionals who specialize in various technical fields such as software development, network administration, cybersecurity, and database management, among others. They work together to support and maintain technical infrastructure within an organization, ensuring that all systems are running smoothly and efficiently.

Organizing Committee

An organizing committee is a group of individuals responsible for planning and managing an event or project. This committee typically consists of individuals with diverse skills and expertise, ranging from project management to marketing and logistics. The committee's main objective is to ensure that the event or project runs smoothly and successfully. To achieve this, the committee is responsible for setting the goals and objectives of the project, identifying the necessary resources and budget, and delegating tasks to subcommittees or team members.
